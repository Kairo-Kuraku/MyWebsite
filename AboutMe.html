Who am I?

Hi! Thank you for taking the time to review my website. I am a student currently studying controls engineering currently at Bridgerland Technical College (BTECH). I have minimal industrial controls work experience as of writing this, but I will change that in the coming future. I am motivated about learning controls whether its Allen Bradley PLCs, Click PLC, Arduino, some C/C++, or any other kind of structure to automate processes. In the future, after completing the Controls Engineering certification at BTECH, I want to continue learning more about Controls and Data Analytics.

My hobbies and career life do have an overlap, I do enjoy home automation and making small projects for automating things in my home and personal life, but also love to spend time indoors and outdoors alike. Whether it be golfing, rock climbing, or video games, I believe the best way to enjoy my free time is with my friends.
