About This Page

This page is where I keep a record of some of the projects I've worked on or currently working on. This includes personal projects I've made as well as any professional projects that I'm able to share. Currently, as I've just started documenting them, there is very few, but in the future I hope to see this page grow large and get better each and every time it does. I mainly started doing this as a way to learn different things, but also it was a great way to combine my enjoyment of electronics, automated systems, and 3d modeling. All of these things I've found very enjoyable and I can make each one shine in its own way for each project I do. I hope you enjoy seeing reading about them as I had making them and maybe help you in whatever you may be doing.
