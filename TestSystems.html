Test Systems

Portable testing carts or Mechanical and Controls Engineers

Overview

This project was an intern project for JR Automation Setpoint division in Ogden, UT. The purpose of this project was to create 2 new test "carts" for both the mechanical and controls engineers to test parts and systems before the build phase of a project. The team of Interns and I worked on designing the test stations working with our customer (Setpoint) to make sure they were to their liking and solved the probelem they had.
